20 Years Later: The Lasting Impact

Ruby's testing culture pioneered concepts that became industry standards: Test-Driven Development, Behavior-Driven Development, readable test syntax, and testing as documentation. These practices spread across languages and frameworks, fundamentally changing how software is built.
